## Strategic principles surfaced

### What makes a real niche
A real niche names 3 specifics in one sentence:
1. **WHO** — specific audience
2. **WHAT** — specific pain or desire
3. **WHERE** — specific channel to reach them

Tests it must pass:
- TAM: 5k–500k people
- Reachable through ONE channel
- Willingness to pay: €50+/month recurring OR €500+ one-time
- No dominant incumbent

### What VC-backed companies ignore (good for 1–3 person teams)
- Boring compliance (ANAF, e-Factura, EU VAT)
- Vertical SaaS for fragmented Romanian industries
- Cross-border services requiring two-culture fluency
- Sub-niches inside high-demand categories
- Service-with-software-layer models (not pure marketplaces)

### Information ≠ business
"An app that tells you information" almost never becomes a business. Information is the cheapest commodity in 2026. Build on transactions, recurring service, or operational complexity — not on facts.

### Service-first beats marketplace-first
Two-sided marketplaces need 18+ months of cash burn to reach liquidity. A service business with software layer generates revenue from customer #1 and accumulates network/data over time.

## Tax advisory — first 90 days (the recommended starting point)

**Days 1–30: Validation**
- Use own returns as case study #1
- 3 pro-bono diaspora friends → testimonials
- Post in 10 diaspora Facebook groups: "Are you confused about Romania-US taxes?"
- Goal: 30+ "yes, I need help" replies

**Days 31–60: Productize**
- Pricing tiers: Simple return €300, Complex (LLC owner) €1,500–3,000, Full Year retainer €5k
- Onboarding: questionnaire + secure document upload
- Partner with 1 Romanian fiscal consultant + 1 US CPA for cross-referrals
- Set up Stripe/Wise for EUR/USD billing
- Build Romanian + English website

**Days 61–90: Launch**
- Soft launch to diaspora network
- Speaking spot in 2 diaspora podcasts/YouTube channels
- Goal: 10 paying clients × €600 avg = €6k in revenue by day 90

## EU market entry for US small businesses — service stack

**Productized "EU Launch Package" — €3,000–5,000 one-time:**
- Romanian SRL formation (cheapest EU jurisdiction, 16% corp tax or 1–3% micro)
- EU VAT registration (OSS/IOSS)
- GDPR representative service
- EU IOR (Importer of Record) for physical goods
- EU bank account setup
- Compliance handover documents

**Ongoing retainer — €500–1,500/month:**
- VAT filings + reconciliation
- GDPR DPO duties
- Annual accounts + corporate filings
- EU regulatory monitoring (DSA, AI Act, EPR updates)

**Upsell layers:**
- EU fulfillment broker (Romanian 3PL warehouse partnership, mark up 25–40%)
- EU customer service outsourcing
- Multi-language storefront setup

**Distribution channels:**
- US Indie Hackers, Twitter founders, MicroConf, Trends.vc
- LinkedIn: search "expanding to Europe" / "EU launch"
- Cold outreach to US Shopify stores $1–10M revenue, no .eu domain
- Partnership with 1–2 US fractional CFOs serving DTC brands
