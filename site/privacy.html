Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. What we collect

Vetera is currently a no-account, no-cookie research tool. We do not require you to register, sign in, or provide personal information to use the search.

If you contact us by email (corrections, questions), we collect only what you send us.

2. What we don't collect

  • We do not track or store your search queries.
  • We do not sell or share any user data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics that profile you.

3. Third-party services

When you click external links (FL Board, OIG LEIE, NPI Registry, SAM.gov), you leave Vetera and become subject to those sites' privacy policies.

4. Cookies

The site uses no tracking cookies. Browser-internal session storage may temporarily hold form input but is cleared on tab close.

5. Information about healthcare providers

The information we display about healthcare providers is sourced exclusively from public records published by U.S. federal and state government agencies for the explicit purpose of public access. Healthcare licensees have a reduced privacy expectation regarding their professional licensing and regulatory record.

If you are a provider and wish to dispute or correct an entry, see our corrections process.

6. Children

Vetera is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted with a new "last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions: veterareports@gmail.com
